## 📊 PSYCHOLOGICAL SCORING METHODOLOGY

Each microinteraction was evaluated on 10 factors (10 points each):
1. **Anticipation & Reward** - Building expectation before payoff
2. **Immediate Feedback** - Response within 100ms
3. **Natural Physics** - Organic, believable movement
4. **Surprise & Delight** - Unexpected positive moments
5. **Sense of Control** - User feels in command
6. **Progress Indication** - Clear state communication
7. **Personality Expression** - Brand alignment
8. **Cognitive Load Reduction** - Simplifies complexity
9. **Gamification** - Playful engagement elements
10. **Emotional Resonance** - Creates positive feelings
